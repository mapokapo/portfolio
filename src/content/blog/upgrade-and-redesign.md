I've been working on improving the look and feel of my site, and I'm excited to
share some changes!

First, the **heading component** now has a smooth _typewriter effect_ that
introduces me right from the start. I also added subtle **fade-in animations**
for each section panel as you scroll through the page, making the experience
feel more dynamic and engaging.

On the technical side, I upgraded the project to **Next.js 13** and enabled
additional optimization flags. It turned out to be trickier than expected 😅,
but the payoff should be a lighter, faster website.

More updates will come soon - this is just the beginning!
